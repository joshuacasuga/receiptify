Privacy Policy

By choosing to log in to Wrapped on Demand, you agree to the use of your Spotify username and listening data for your top artists and tracks.


Any data displayed by Wrapped on Demand is neither stored, collected, nor shared with any third parties anywhere. All data is solely used to display your listening statistics.


Rest assured that your data is NOT being stored or being used in any malicious manner.


If you choose to revoke permissions for Wrapped on Demand to access your data, you can visit your Manage Apps page and click "Remove Access" on Wrapped on Demand.
